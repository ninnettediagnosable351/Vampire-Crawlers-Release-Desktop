## ⚙️ Game Settings

Adjust your game settings from the Options menu to improve visual quality or performance. You can change the resolution to match your monitor settings. Use 4K settings if your hardware supports it for the best pixel art fidelity. Toggle V-Sync to prevent screen tearing during fast movement. Adjust music and sound effects volumes using the sliders provided in the audio tab.

## ⚔️ Gameplay Mechanics

Each run introduces new monsters and challenges. Move your character using the WASD keys. Your character attacks monsters automatically based on the weapons and items you collect. Every time you level up, you choose a new ability or stat boost. Collect experience gems dropped by fallen enemies to gain levels. Survive against larger waves as the clock ticks toward dawn.

## 🛡️ Troubleshooting

If the game fails to start, verify your graphics card drivers are current. You can update these through your computer manufacturer’s website. If the game feels slow, reduce the resolution or disable high-definition particle effects in the settings menu.

Contact your operating system support if you encounter persistent installation errors. Ensure your user account holds administrator privileges to allow the software to create the necessary files on your hard drive.
